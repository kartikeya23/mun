The Comittees

PM's Cabinet

Agenda: Combatting the spread of Covid-19 in India by strategising a quarantine after understanding it's significance and impacts.


This Cabinet is going to explore the aspects of quarantining that majority of the victims of lockdown ignore. So, Delegates of the Prime Minister’s Cabinet, prepare yourselves for a debate that is stimulating, impactful and most importantly, enlightening. We look forward to witnessing constructive arguments from all you!

UN Security Council

Agenda: Investigating the prospect of COVID-19 being a bioweapon engineered by the PRC


The COVID-19 pandemic has led to countless allegations agains the People's Republic of China stating that the novel coronavirus is a bioweapon engineered to create an economic vacuum for the Chinese to fill. It is for the delegates of the Security Council to determine.

ECOFIN

Agenda: Addressing global economic policy going forward in regards to the impact of Covid-19, with special emphasis on sustainable growth.


Covid-19 has sent stock markets and growth rates tumbling, disrupted global supply chains and forced governments to dole out unprecedented stimulus packages. How will the global economy navigate these challenges and make a successful recovery? It’s for the delegates of ECOFIN to determine.

World Health Organisation

Establishing means to contain the spread of Covid-19, discussing the development of a vaccine and building improved public health systems to avoid future outbreaks.


The World Health Organization is on the frontlines in the war against the coronavirus. In the past, it has played a leading role in several public health achievements from the eradication of smallpox, the near eradication of polio and the development of the Ebola vaccine. At eJPIS MUN 2020, the WHO will work to promptly contain the spread of the coronavirus and develop mechanisms for the same especially in low economically developed nations. Delegates will also be tasked with establishing a framework for the development of a vaccine and facilitating improved public health systems such as those for the People's Republic of China. Prepare yourself for a stimulating yet enlightening debate to rid our world of this deadly pandemic.
